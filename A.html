Let yourself be seduced by the charm and superb beaches of Hammamet! Located on the south-east coast of the Cap Bon peninsula, Hammamet is the "Tunisian Saint-Tropez", in a green setting with lemon and orange orchards. You can also stroll through the markets in search of beautiful local pottery and stroll through the old town (Medina) whose surrounding walls date back 1500 years. In summer, it's the time for music and theater festivals, not to be missed under any circumstances!
